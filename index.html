A space to develop fundemental CS skills

At the heart of computing are fundemental technical skills, and the arts implicit in these skills are sound problem solving & reasoning skills. Weather it be in the design of large scale distributed systems, mobile applications, machine learning, or networking, the thought process and problem solving steps involved largely stay the same.

This study space includes notes on a variety of technical topics, including a well-defined set of resources that not only act as a collection of technical content but also as a blueprint to build & improve skills.
