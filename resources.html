Roadmap to build new skills

Find a topic of interest, dig deep and make sure you learn the ins and outs of that topic. It’s then important to practice by solving problems pertaining to the topic to learn to apply the skills you’ve picked up. The key here is to make sure you practice with the intent to improve. Make sure you analyze your problem-solving approach, explore other possible solutions and reason your way through it.

Repetition is key. Sometimes it is not just enough to understand how an algorithm works. It’s not enough to only learn an algorithm, understand how it works and stop there. It's important to practice(repeatedly) the different ways that problem can manifest itself. At the same time, it’s also very important to keep progressing. If you feel quite comfortable with a problem, and its different variations, make sure to progress and keep challenging yourself.
