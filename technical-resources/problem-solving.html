Steps to build strong data structure & algorithm skills

1

Learn

Understand in-depth key foundational topics such as data strucutres, algorithms, & basic math skills. Don't progress to the next topic without being comfortable with current topic(things tend to build on top of one another).
2

Practice

Practice by solving various problems. Repetition is key to become familliar with problem solving techniques and to become familiar with common patterns.
3

Progress

Challenging problems are good. They present an oppurtunity to learn something new. So make sure there is progression in the difficulty level and scale of the problems.
